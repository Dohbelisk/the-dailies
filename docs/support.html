Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any premium subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

Why can't I see today's puzzles?

New puzzles are released daily at midnight in your local timezone. Make sure you have an internet connection to download the latest puzzles. You can also try pulling down to refresh on the home screen.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings on your iPhone > tap your name > Subscriptions > The Dailies > Cancel Subscription.

Is my progress saved?

Yes! Your progress is saved locally on your device and synced to our servers when you're signed in. Create an account to ensure your progress is backed up.

How do hints work?

You get 3 free hints per day. You can earn additional hints by watching a short video ad, or get unlimited hints with a premium subscription.

Can I play past puzzles?

Yes! Access the Archive from the home screen. Free users can access limited past puzzles using tokens, while premium users have unlimited archive access.
